EventMapper

OBJECTIVE:

To develop a web and mobile platform for creating map based guides to allow users to mange and share indoor location-based information in a “point and click” manner.

INNOVATION AND BENEFITS:
  • Provide on-the-fly customizable map and location information
  • Serve as the map archive for hotels and exhibition venues that can be packaged for use by conference organizers to create map enhanced conference program information
  • Enhance OneMap, an integrated map system for government agencies to deliver location-based services and information
APPLICATIONS:
  • Map-based content authoring
  • Publish and distribute the map that can be accessed from both desktop and mobile devices
Grant Awarded: SLA GeoInnovation Fund
Duration: 1 Oct 2015 to 30 Sep 2016
Team: Zin Ko Ko Min
